# CSSRotate: angle property

{{APIRef("CSS Typed OM")}}

The **`angle`** property of the
{{domxref("CSSRotate")}} interface gets and sets the angle of rotation. A positive angle
denotes a clockwise rotation, a negative angle a counter-clockwise one.
